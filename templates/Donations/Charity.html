Save the Dinosaurs

We believe that the dinosaurs had their shot at being alive unfairly taken from them by the KT extinction. We want to bring them back! Save the dinosaurs! For more information, go to Save the Dinosaurs.

Combjellies Are People Too!

Most people look at a combjelly and think, "What's that gross blob on the bottom of my sneaker?" Our charity raises awareness about important combjelly issues in today's politically correct climate. For more information, go to Combjellies Are People Too!.

World Multistrata Agroforestry Fund

Multistrata agroforestry has the potential to sequester billions of tons of carbon from the atmosphere, but it costs a looooot of money. Support climate change reversal and healthy farming practices by donating to the WMAF! or more information, go to WMAF.

Washed Up Movie Stars Rehab Fund

Every day, dozens of actresses and actors realize they have no talent or money and have been coasting off the fame they received from acting in that movie or TV show that you watched an episode of when you were really bored that one time and can't remember the name of now because it was terrible. If you donate to our charity, you can help give these down-on-their-luck stars the second chance they need to try and become functioning adults! or more information, go to WUMSRF.
