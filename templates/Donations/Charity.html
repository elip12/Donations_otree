Santa Cruz Humane Society

The Santa Cruz Humane Society is a no kill shelter that provides compassionate care and adoption services for homeless dogs and cats in Nogales, Arizona. For more information, go to Santa Cruz Humane Society.

Santa Cruz Education Foundation

We believe every child deserves an excellent neighborhood school. The Santa Cruz Education Foundation (SCEF) supports Santa Cruz city schools (K-12) through advocacy, outreach and fundraising. For more information, go to Santa Cruz Education Foundation.

Año Nuevo Research at UCSC

Año Nuevo Reserve is one of the University of California's 39 Natural Reserves. The close proximity of the Reserve to UC Santa Cruz allows students hands-on research experience while working on world-class scientific research projects. Recent research topics include contaminant bioaccumulation in top predators, the impact of acoustic disturbance on marine mammals, etc. For more information, go to Año Nuevo Research at UCSC.
